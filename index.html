Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission: The mission of Strathmore University is to provide all-round quality education through excellence in teaching, research and scholarship, in an atmosphere of freedom and responsibility, focusing on ethical service to society.

Our Vision: To be a leading entrepreneurial university, positively impacting society by doing excellent work.

About Strathmore University

Strathmore University is a leading non-profit private university in Kenya, with a global reputation for excellence in teaching and research. We are committed to ethical practices, service to society, and fostering an environment where students can achieve their full potential.

Achievements

Strathmore University has established itself as a premier institution recognized locally and internationally. Over the years, it has received numerous accolades, including:

  • Ranked among the top private universities in East Africa by uniRank and Webometrics.
  • Awarded the ICT Hall of Fame Award for contributions to digital transformation in education.
  • Accreditation from global professional bodies such as ACCA, CFA Institute, and CISCO.
  • Successful alumni network with graduates thriving in leadership positions globally.
  • First university in Kenya to achieve a Green Building Certification for its environmentally conscious infrastructure.

Ongoing Projects

Strathmore University is actively involved in numerous projects that align with its strategic goals in academics, research, and student development. Current initiatives include:

Academic Projects

  • Expansion of online learning platforms and creation of micro-credentials to enhance accessibility and flexibility.
  • Curriculum modernization through interdisciplinary programs blending technology, ethics, and entrepreneurship.

Research Projects

  • Energy Research Centre: Conducting sustainable energy projects including solar grid optimization and clean energy policy advisories.
  • AI and Data Analytics Hub: Exploring AI-driven solutions for healthcare, education, and financial services in Africa.

Student-Centered Projects

  • Mentorship programs connecting students with professionals across various industries.
  • Launch of the Strathmore Innovation Lab (SiL) to support startups and student entrepreneurship.
  • Community engagement projects focused on digital literacy and social responsibility in underprivileged areas.

Through these initiatives, the University remains committed to innovation, impactful research, and empowering the next generation of leaders.
